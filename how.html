How it works

Our community of expert developers is here to help you solve your coding problem easily and fast.


 

One click away!

Just one click on our app button, and you'll find the list of avaialble experts in the relevant field you need asistance with, sort them out assisting their Github & Stackoverflow profiles and once you have chosen an expert, we connect the two of you over online session in which you can chat, talk, use video and obviously share desktops in order to go deeper into your code.
Have the session going till you feel your problem have been solved.

Taking notes

There is no need for taking notes, all sessions are recroded and would be available for you anytime, in your Floatward account

Billing

You are getting billed by minutes, not by hour, so you acually pay on what you've consumed, with minimum waste

 

Validate your consultant

To enable you to make sure you have found the right match, we only charge sessions longer that two minutes, so use this feature wisely.

Exceeding your plan capacity

Once the usage will get close to the maximum per your pricing plan, we'll notify you, and ask for your further instructions.

Set objectives

It is very important to set to objective of every session, let your consultant know what are looking for, solving a problem, reviewing a piece of code, optimizing process or other.

Best practice

Floatward is not a replacement to a simple Google or Stackoverflow search, we believe you should use our service when you got to a dead end, when you need immediate and interactive response or when you need someone else to with you, otherwise it would make more sense to use the common practice.
